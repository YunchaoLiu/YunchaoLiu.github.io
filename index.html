Short Bio

Yunchao, nicknamed Lance, is currently a PhD student at the Meiler lab where he is co-advised by Dr. Jens Meiler and Dr. Bobby Bodenheimer, and also mentored by Dr. Tyler Derr. His research aims at developing novel state-of-the-art deep learning models that are specifically designed for small molecule drug discovery applications.
